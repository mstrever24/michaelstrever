About

Audio that keeps the image alive.

I work with directors, producers, agencies, and artists who need dependable sound at every stage of a project. On set, that means recording dialogue, ambience, movement, and location texture with care. In post, it means shaping those materials into a clear, emotional, release-ready track.

My process is practical and story-led: protect the performance, build the world around it, and make sure the final mix feels natural, detailed, and intentional.

Process
01

Prep the sound plan

Review the script, schedule, locations, references, and delivery needs so the audio workflow supports the production from day one.

02

Record on location

Capture clean dialogue, useful ambience, and practical sonic material while staying responsive to the rhythm of the shoot.

03

Edit, clean, and design

Shape the recorded material, repair problems, build detail, and create the sound world the picture needs.

04

Mix and deliver

Balance dialogue, music, effects, and ambience into a final mix with clear deliverables for release.
