Field recordings / location textures

Specific places, recorded as sound.

A growing archive of location sounds: concrete rooms, mountain air, street edges, interiors, water, wind, and the small details that make a place feel alive.

Sound log
01 Exterior / wind / rock

Table Mountain wind through stone

A close recording of wind moving across rock, scrub, and exposed concrete-like surfaces for texture beds and transitions.

02 Urban / night / distance

Late street ambience after traffic

Distant vehicles, soft room spill, footsteps, and night air for documentary beds, quiet city scenes, and transitional space.

03 Interior / concrete / resonance

Empty stairwell tone

A static recording of interior reflections, distant building movement, and subtle concrete resonance for room tone and design.

04 Water / harbour / mechanical

Harbour water against metal

Water slap, distant ropes, metal ticks, and low mechanical hum for atmosphere, tension, and environmental storytelling.

Recording approach

Each recording is treated as a usable story element.

Files can be captured clean and natural, or shaped into designed textures for film, documentary, installation, and branded work. Add your finished audio files to the location sounds folder and the page will render them as playable audio previews.
